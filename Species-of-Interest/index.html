Meet the birds

The birds below are the species of interest for the SaPP project. They have been selected due to their appearance in academic literature flower-foraging, as well as through observations by members of the public. While, ideally, we would sample birds throughout the United States, we are focusing on states along the Pacific Coast of North America: the western flyway. We ask that collaborators collect pollen from hummingbirds when possible, for potential comparisons of flowering species preference between songbirds and hummingbirds.
